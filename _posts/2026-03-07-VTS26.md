---
title: New paper on feature-aware Trojan alteration
---

Our new hardware security paper uses ML explainability analysis to alter hardware Trojans to avoid detection by ML-based model. It is accepted for publication at IEEE VLSI Test Symposium as late breaking result. Special congratulations to wiscad members and student authors [Lizi Zhang](https://wiscad.github.io/wiscad/members/lizi-zhang.html) and [Navid Nader Tehrani](https://wiscad.github.io/wiscad/members/navid-nadertehrani.html).
